Student Master File

The file typically includes personal information such as name, address, and contact information, as well as academic information such as enrollment status, major, GPA, and transcripts.

Student Ledger of Accounts

A student ledger of accounts is a financial record that contains detailed information about the financial transactions of a student at a school, college, or university.

Enrollment & Registration

Registration is the process of selecting and scheduling classes for a specific semester or term, after a student is enrolled.

Deposit Slip Upload

The deposit slip is a form that is used to document the deposit of cash or check into a bank account.

Student Accountabilities

Student accountabilities refer to the responsibilities and obligations that a student has to a school, college, or university.

Individual Evaluation Report

An individual evaluation report is a document that provides a detailed assessment of an individual's performance, skills, and abilities.

Faculty Performance Evaluation

Faculty performance evaluation is the process of assessing and evaluating the effectiveness and quality of a faculty member's work at a school, college, or university.

Request for Subject Grade Completion

request for subject grade completion refers to a request submitted by a student to their school, college, or university, asking for an incomplete grade for a course to be completed and a final grade to be assigned.

Online Clearance Request

An online clearance request refers to a process of requesting permission or approval to proceed with a specific activity or task through an online system.

Display Own Grades

Display own grade refers to the process of allowing students to view and access their own grades for a particular class, course or semester through an online portal or system.

Apply for Graduation

Applying for graduation is the process of formally requesting to receive a diploma or degree from a school, college, or university.

Change Own Password

Change password refers to the process of updating or replacing a current password with a new one.
